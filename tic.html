Tic Tac Toe

Player O's Turn

Click on an empty square to place your mark. First to get 3 in a row wins!
How to Play
  • Two players take turns placing X or O.
  • The first to align 3 in a row
    (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins.
  • If all 9 cells are filled with no winner,
    the game is a draw.
  • Click "New Game" to reset the board.
